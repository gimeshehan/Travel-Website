Little Adam's Peak

Little Adam's Peak is a popular hiking destination located near the town of Ella in Sri Lanka. Rising about 1141 meters (3740 feet) above sea level, this relatively easy hike offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding lush landscapes, tea plantations, and valleys. The trail takes around 30-45 minutes to ascend to the summit, meandering through picturesque tea fields and small forested areas. Once at the top, hikers are rewarded with breathtaking vistas, making it a favorite spot for travelers seeking beautiful scenery and a relatively accessible hiking experience in the Ella region.
