Narangala

Narangala is a captivating mountain peak situated in the Badulla District of Sri Lanka, offering panoramic views and an enchanting trekking experience. Rising approximately 1,432 meters (4,698 feet) above sea level, Narangala rewards adventurers with stunning vistas of misty mountains, verdant valleys, and sprawling tea estates. The trek to the summit is a moderately challenging yet rewarding journey, taking hikers through lush forests, remote villages, and scenic landscapes. At the top, visitors are treated to a breathtaking 360-degree view, making Narangala a hidden gem for nature lovers seeking captivating vistas and a memorable hiking adventure in Sri Lanka.
