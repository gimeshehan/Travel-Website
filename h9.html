Diyaluma Ella

Diyaluma Ella stands as one of Sri Lanka's tallest waterfalls, boasting a mesmerizing drop of approximately 220 meters (722 feet). Situated near the town of Koslanda, it offers a breathtaking sight as the water plunges down rocky cliffs into a series of cascading pools below. The trek to Diyaluma Ella can be an adventurous journey, navigating through forests and rocky terrain, but the reward of reaching its base or the top tiers is truly awe-inspiring. The natural beauty and the opportunity to swim in the refreshing pools make Diyaluma Ella a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers visiting the region.
