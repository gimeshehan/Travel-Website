Bomburu Ella

Bomburu Ella, also known as Bomburu Falls, is a stunning waterfall nestled in the hill country of Sri Lanka, near the town of Nuwara Eliya. It's renowned for its natural beauty and captivating scenery. The falls cascade down from a height of around 50 meters (164 feet) in multiple tiers, creating a picturesque sight amidst the lush greenery of the surrounding forest. Accessible via a trek through scenic landscapes and tea plantations, Bomburu Ella offers visitors a tranquil and refreshing experience, making it a popular stop for nature enthusiasts and travelers exploring the captivating beauty of Sri Lanka's waterfalls.
