Dunhinda Ella

Dunhinda Ella, located near Badulla in Sri Lanka, is a majestic waterfall renowned for its striking beauty and dramatic cascade. Tumbling down from a height of around 64 meters (210 feet), the waterfall is surrounded by dense forests and picturesque landscapes, creating a captivating sight for visitors. Accessible through a short trek along a scenic path, Dunhinda Ella is a popular attraction, especially during the rainy season when the waterfall is at its most impressive. The misty spray and thunderous sound of the cascading water add to the allure of this natural wonder, making it a must-visit destination for those exploring Sri Lanka's enchanting waterfalls.
