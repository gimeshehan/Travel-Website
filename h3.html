Ravana Falls

Ravana Falls, also known as Ravana Ella, is a breathtaking waterfall nestled amidst the lush greenery near Ella in Sri Lanka. Legend intertwines with this natural wonder, as it is said to be associated with the epic Indian tale Ramayana. The waterfall cascades down from an impressive height of approximately 25 meters (82 feet), creating a picturesque sight as it plunges into a pool below. Surrounded by dense foliage and rocky outcrops, Ravana Falls offers a serene and scenic atmosphere, enticing visitors to enjoy its beauty and refreshing waters. Accessible via a short hike and offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, Ravana Falls is a popular stop for travelers exploring the enchanting beauty of Sri Lanka's natural attractions.
