Nine Arch

The Nine Arch Bridge, situated near Ella in Sri Lanka, is an architectural marvel enveloped by lush greenery and picturesque landscapes. This iconic bridge, constructed during the British colonial era, is renowned for its stunning design comprising nine exquisite arches made of bricks, stones, and cement without the use of steel. Set against the backdrop of dense forests and tea plantations, it offers a captivating sight as the railway trains pass over it, creating a timeless and picturesque scene. The bridge has become a popular attraction, drawing visitors who marvel at its structural elegance and surrounding natural beauty, making it a must-visit spot for tourists exploring Ella and its surroundings in Sri Lanka.
