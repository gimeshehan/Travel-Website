Ella Rock

Ella Rock, a prominent peak near the town of Ella in Sri Lanka, is a sought-after destination for nature lovers and adventurers. The hike to its summit unveils a mesmerizing landscape of rolling hills, lush tea plantations, and picturesque valleys. The trail, winding through verdant forests and rural villages, takes around 2-3 hours to complete. Reaching the top rewards hikers with breathtaking 360-degree views, showcasing the stunning beauty of Sri Lanka's hill country. Ella Rock offers an immersive outdoor experience, combining a moderately challenging trek with spectacular panoramas, making it an unmissable highlight for visitors exploring this region.
